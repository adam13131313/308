SYDNEY MARATHON PWA - INSTALL IN 5 MINUTES
===========================================

WHAT YOU HAVE
A complete Progressive Web App. Once hosted, it installs to your phone's
home screen, runs full-screen like a native app, works offline, and saves
your runs permanently on your device.

EASIEST HOSTING: NETLIFY DROP (free, no account, ~2 min)
--------------------------------------------------------
1. On a computer, go to:  https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag the ENTIRE "marathon-pwa" folder onto the page.
3. Netlify gives you a URL like  https://random-name-123.netlify.app
4. Open that URL on your phone.

ADD TO HOME SCREEN
------------------
iPhone (Safari):
  - Tap the Share button (square with up arrow)
  - Scroll down, tap "Add to Home Screen"
  - Tap "Add"

Android (Chrome):
  - Tap the three-dot menu
  - Tap "Add to Home screen" / "Install app"
  - Tap "Install"

You'll get the "3:09 SYD" icon on your home screen. Open it and it runs
full-screen with no browser bars.

USING IT
--------
- PLAN tab: tap any week to expand the 7 days. Tap an empty run day to log it.
  Easy/long runs faster than 5:10/km get flagged orange (too fast).
- CHART tab: cumulative km, easy-run HR trend, weekly volume.
- METRICS tab: VO2max, LT pace, and your MP/LT headroom.

Your logged runs save automatically on your phone (localStorage) and persist
between sessions and offline.

OTHER HOSTING OPTIONS
---------------------
- Vercel:  https://vercel.com  (drag folder, free, needs account)
- GitHub Pages: push folder to a repo, enable Pages in settings
- Cloudflare Pages: similar to Netlify

UPDATING DATA LATER
-------------------
The 30 runs logged so far are baked in as the starting point. Anything you
add via the app is stored on your phone on top of that. If you want me to
refresh the baked-in baseline later (e.g. after we log more in the
spreadsheet), I can regenerate the files and you re-drop them on Netlify.
